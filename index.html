FAQ

Questions founders ask first.

Can AI really get this right?+
Every number is solver-checked, unit-checked and re-derived a second way by an independent verifier — and a human signs off as the final gate. You can start with a free Feasibility Teaser and judge the quality risk-free before paying anything.
What exactly do I receive?+
A complete, traceable design package: configuration, sizing, aerodynamics, propulsion, performance envelope, first-pass stability & structures, parametric CAD, an India-available bill of materials, and a certification-readiness note — formatted as a report with a traceability appendix.
Is this a substitute for full FEA/CFD or flight testing?+
No — and we say so explicitly. Forge EI delivers an internally consistent, physics-validated, fully traceable conceptual-and-preliminary design. Full FEA/CFD, manufacturing drawings and DGCA flight-cert testing are downstream and out of scope. The deliverable is a design study for your own further engineering and testing.
How fast is delivery?+
A Conceptual Design lands in 2–4 days, the Preliminary & Detailed Design in 1–2 weeks, and the Complete Package is scoped to your mission. The free Feasibility Teaser is typically same-day.
How does payment work?+
50% upfront and 50% on delivery for new clients. Scope and price are fixed up front — one design plus two revision rounds. Anything beyond that is a change order.
I already design in-house. Why you?+
Use Forge EI as overflow capacity, a specific variant, or a fast second opinion before you commit to tooling — without the cost of hiring a full-time aero team.
